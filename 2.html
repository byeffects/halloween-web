Information about the halloween party

.It is a feast created by the Celts, great bonfires are lit on the mountains to destroy the evil spirits

.That's why no light was lit in the houses, and people dressed as witches or demons so that no soul would want to dwell on their bodies

.A well-known custom is the trick or treatment that means to go to the door of the houses and ask for sweets disguised as something related to terror

.They also decorate the houses and places known in the city.

.With the arrival of Christianity, it was established on the first day of November as the Day of All Saints and it is as well as day to day we celebrate this day halloween.

.There are also special halloween meals such as caramelized apple, Caramel with shapes related to the world of dead: skull, bones, worms, etc. Toffee confectionery, pieces of hard caramel made from sugar cane
